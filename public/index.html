About

Blockdays is a daily puzzle game set on a calendar, and developed by Ian McKenna. The objective of the game is to fit all the pieces onto the Calendar, fitting around the blocked squares. The blocked squares change each day to match the new date, creating a new puzzle for each calendar day.

How to Play:

  • Select a piece from the Piece Palette at the bottom.
  • Use the 4 control buttons to orient the selected piece as desired.
    • Rotate CCW, Flip X, Flip Y, Rotate CW
  • Drag and drop the piece from the Piece View onto the calendar.
  • You may drag and drop pieces from the Calendar as desired as well.
  • Pieces cannot overlap with each other or with the blocked squares.
  • Use the "Reset Game" button to clear the board if you get stuck.
